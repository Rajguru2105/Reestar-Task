Japanese Cuisine: Sushi

Sushi is a Japanese dish consisting of vinegared rice accompanied by various ingredients such as seafood, vegetables, and occasionally tropical fruits. Common types include nigiri (rice topped with seafood).

Mexican Cuisine: Tacos

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish made from small hand-sized corn or wheat tortillas topped with a filling. Common fillings include beef, pork, chicken, seafood, beans, and vegetables.

Thai Cuisine: Pad Thai

Pad Thai is a stir-fried rice noodle dish commonly served as street food and at casual local eateries in Thailand. It typically includes eggs, tofu, shrimp, peanuts, a tangy tamarind sauce, and lime wedges.

Italian Cuisine: Spaghetti

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Roman pasta dish made with eggs, Pecorino Romano cheese, pancetta, and pepper. The creamy sauce is created by tossing hot pasta with raw eggs and cheese.
